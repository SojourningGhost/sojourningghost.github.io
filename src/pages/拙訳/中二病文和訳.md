---
title: 中二病文和訳
---

I was commissioned several years ago to translate the following text from English into Japanese. It was written to be as ｜厨二《chuuni》, i.e. edgy, as possible. Since I was not asked to edit the original English script, I did not. I have since made some minor adjustments to my original translation to bring it closer to my current standard.

Deciding what to do with *brimstone* gave me pause. *Brimstone* in English is a compound of the old forms of *burn* and *stone*. It literally translates to ｜硫黄《sulfur》, but I was worried the religious connotation would be lost, so I briefly considered using ｜燃え石《burn stone》 as a calque. Fortunately, I found that it was rendered 硫黄 in both the ｜文語訳聖書《literary version》 and in a more modern translation of the Bible, so the nuance remains in Japanese for those with eyes to see.

---

I'm not delusional. 

What I speak now is the absolute truth, not some childish fantasy. 

From the instant I was thrown from my mother's womb with the umbilical cord wrapped around my neck, it became apparent that human consciousness is a waste of our world's finite resources; developed as what I can only hope is a joke.

The human spirit is constantly chewed in the cogs of false evolution. In a vain attempt to survive, you accept your place. Adapting into that of which life demands: nothing but a cog turning the wheels of fate.

Me, on the other hand, my consciousness--my SOUL--is in it's natural shape: resembling that of barbed wire. 

I looked at those who contorted and writhed, and forged my own path. I will not conceal the beast within me, my nature.

As a grim reaper--no, THE grim reaper, this is supposed to be a job that's done in the name of justice. To maintain the good and evil in the world. To ensure proper punishment for the ultimate waste of our most precious resource: life.

I can't say I subscribe to this idea; I just like killing people.

No matter how trivial something may seem, it has the potential to shape the future.

With what I'm capable of, I understand exactly why they've attempted to limit my abilities. The methods they've used to hold me back however, have grossly miscalculated for what I'm about to do.

Do you know what happens to those who rebel against nature's wishes?

If you don't, allow me to demonstrate. The examples I provide will be documented for all of eternity! This is my final chance to restore order.

The brimstone that surrounds me can only try to contain what some call God's greatest mistake.

---

俺は妄想している訳では無い。

今話すのは絶対真実で有り、子供じみた幻想等では無い。

臍の緒が首に巻き付きながら母の胎から投げ出された刹那より、人間の意識は我等が世界の限った資源の浪費で有るという事が明白に為っている。冗談として発達したと云った方が未だ増しだ。

人間の精神は常に偽進化の歯車の中で咬み砕かれている。空しい生存行為として分を受け入れ、人生が強要する物へと変わる。その果ては運命の輪を回転させる歯でしか無い。

一方、我が意識、我が「魂」は有刺鉄線に似た、自然の形をしているのだ。

歪み悶える者を見て、自分の道を作った。天性という内なる獣は隠さないのだ。

死神として、否、唯一無二の死神としてこれは正義の名の下に行われる仕事の筈で有る。この世の善と悪を保つ為に。「生命」という、我等が最も貴重な資源の無駄遣いに対して正しい罰が下される事を保障する為に。

が、こういう理念に賛同しているとは言えないな。俺は唯、人を殺すのが好きなだけだ。

どんなに些細そうな物事でも、未来を変える可能性を秘めている。

俺の出来る事の前に、何故俺の能力を限ろうとしたか良く理解する。然し、俺を抑える為に使った手段は今しようとしている事に対して大きな誤算なのだ。

大自然の望みに叛逆する者達に何がされるか、知っているのか？

知らないのであれば、示そうでは無いか。俺が提供する実例は永遠に記録されるのだ！これは秩序を回復する、俺の最後の機会なのだ。

俺を囲んでいる硫黄に出来るのは神様の最大の間違いと呼ばれる者を封じようとするだけだ。
